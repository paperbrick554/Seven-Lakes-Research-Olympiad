About SLROC
Curiosity and innovation thrive when students are given the freedom to explore beyond the classroom.
Mission

SLROC was founded on the belief that meaningful research opportunities are often overlooked in high school. We created a space where inquiry, collaboration, and discovery are central — a place to pursue real questions, share knowledge, and build skills that carry beyond the classroom. Our goal is to empower students to become active contributors to knowledge, not just consumers of it.

How It Works

Members engage in weekly discussions, present ideas, and collaborate on research projects throughout the year. Officers lead meetings, organize events, and connect members with resources and mentors. The whole structure is designed so that anyone — regardless of background — can show up on a Tuesday and leave feeling like a researcher.

Beyond the Research

SLROC is also a community. We run workshops, host guest speakers, and make time for game nights and social events. The friendships built here tend to outlast the research papers. We want members to look forward to Tuesdays — because this is a room full of people who actually think deeply about things.
